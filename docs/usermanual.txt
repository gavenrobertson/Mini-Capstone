-- Submitting a 4392 --
Run from main.py
Then fill in your student information
Once done press next
Then fill in your information for submitting your 4392
Once done press submit

-- Viewing the 4392's in MTL Viewer -- 
Run from mtl.py
It will display the list of active 4392's that have been submitted
Click on a 4392 and a new display window will show up giving the details relating to that 4392
Press close once finished viewing the details window
To delete, highlight the 4392 and then press delete selected row
